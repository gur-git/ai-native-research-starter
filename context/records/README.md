# context/records/

What gates and decisions leave behind: gate transcript summaries and stamps, researcher overrides (one line each — including a **researcher-overridden** close, where the researcher chose to skip a gate after the agent explained the stake), decision records (what was chosen, what was rejected, why — outcomes attached as they arrive), and brief **felt-vs-measured** notes (where the researcher's sense of how fast/helpful/well-understood something was diverged from what the records show). Agent-maintained, dated files (`YYYY-MM-DD-<slug>.md`). This is the raw material for the periodic review conversation about how the researcher and the work are developing — no separate ledgers exist or need to.
